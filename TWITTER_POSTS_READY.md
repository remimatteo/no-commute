## Quick Twitter Tips

### Getting Job URLs the Easy Way:
1. Go to your homepage
2. **RIGHT-CLICK** on any job
3. **"Copy link address"**
4. That's the full URL!

### Best Times to Post:
- **Morning:** 9-10 AM (your timezone)
- **Afternoon:** 1-2 PM
- **Evening:** 5-6 PM

### Engagement Tips:
- Reply to people who comment
- Like and RT relevant posts
- Follow people who engage with you
- Use 2-3 hashtags per tweet
- Mix job posts with tips/insights

### What's Working:
- Job posts with salary ($$$)
- Personal stories
- Quick tips (bullet points)
- Questions (engagement)

### What to Avoid:
- Posting same thing twice
- Too many hashtags (looks spammy)
- Only promotional content
- Buying followers
